Compatibility summary:

- Customer cards are supported. Customer card endpoints are documented by Vandar and covered by package contract tests.
- `CustomerResource::authenticationKyc()`
- `CustomerResource::authenticationShahkar()`
- `CustomerResource::cashInCode()`
- `CustomerResource::deleteCashInCode()`
- `SubscriptionResource::createAuthorization()`
- `SubscriptionResource::createWithdrawal()`
- `SubscriptionResource::createRefund()`
- Subscription / direct debit endpoints are supported through the SDK/client methods documented in the English and Persian matrices.
